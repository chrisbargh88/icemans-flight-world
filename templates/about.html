About ICEMAN’s FLIGHT WORLD

Welcome to ICEMAN’s FLIGHT WORLD – where the mullets are optional but the aviators are mandatory. Nestled in the scenic skies above the definitely FAA-certified Ray’s Airfield in Berry NSW, we offer high-octane flight experiences for the dangerously curious.

Whether you're chasing the Gs in a Super Tucano, flying low and slow in a Decathlon, or pulling Top Gun moves in a Mirage F1, we’ve got the wings to match your wildest Maverick dreams.

Our flight training school isn't just about becoming a pilot – it’s about becoming a legend. Think of it as aviation with a side of adrenaline and a soundtrack that slaps. We offer themed flight packages like “Roll & Rumble”, “Sky Patrol”, and the ever-popular “Teach the Sky Instructor Flight ” (aerobatic training for those with strong stomachs and weak self-preservation instincts).

Whether you’re a newbie or a near-ace, we have the aircraft and the attitude to make your day unforgettable. So suit up, grab your callsign, and prepare to buzz the tower. Because at ICEMAN’s FLIGHT WORLD, your altitude is only limited by your attitude.
